Welcome to my research blog!

This site is built with **Next.js**, **TypeScript**, and **MDX**. I'll be posting:

- Long-form blog articles on research topics
- Summaries and write-ups of published papers
- Notes from experiments and projects

## Getting Started

To add a new blog post, create a `.md` or `.mdx` file in `content/blog/` with the following frontmatter:

```yaml
---
title: "Your Post Title"
date: "2026-01-01"
excerpt: "A short description shown on the listing page."
tags: ["tag1", "tag2"]
---
```

Then write your content below the frontmatter in Markdown.
